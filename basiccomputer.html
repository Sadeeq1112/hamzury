Program Overview

Welcome to Hamzury Innovation Hub’s Basic Computer Knowledge program, designed to give students a comprehensive foundation in computing skills. This program is ideal for beginners who are new to computers and want to gain essential skills required in today’s digital world. We offer a single, fully integrated program that combines all the basic computer knowledge necessary to thrive in various fields. There is no advanced version of this course—all the critical concepts are covered within 4 months of intensive training.

Program Structure and Duration

Basic Computer Knowledge Package

  • Duration: 4 months
  • Certificate awarded upon successful completion.
  • The course covers essential topics such as computer operations, Microsoft Office suite, basic internet navigation, file management, email communication, typing skills, and data entry.
  • Students will engage in practical hands-on sessions to ensure they can confidently use computers in personal, academic, and professional settings.
  • Every four weeks, students will have team presentations and collaborative projects to demonstrate their understanding of the material.
  • In the final week, students will complete a capstone project that brings together all their learning, such as creating a document or presentation, sending professional emails, or managing files.
  • All activities, including practicals, teamwork, and assignments, are compulsory.
  • Tuition fees for the program: 45,000 NGN.

Innovation Hub Rules

  • Punctuality: Be on time for all classes and scheduled sessions.
  • Active Participation: Students must fully engage in all assignments, group projects, and activities.
  • Respect for All: Treat instructors, peers, and staff with respect at all times.
  • Meet Deadlines: Assignments, projects, and tasks should be completed and submitted promptly.
  • Academic Integrity: Plagiarism and dishonesty are not tolerated; originality is expected.
  • Professional Behavior: Maintain professional conduct during all practicals and group work sessions.

What You Will Be Able to Do After the Program

By the end of the program, students will have mastered the essential computer skills needed in various professional and academic fields. You will be able to:

  • Operate a computer confidently, including using the Windows or Mac operating systems for day-to-day tasks.
  • Create, edit, and format documents using Microsoft Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.
  • Navigate the internet effectively, perform research, and manage emails using platforms like Gmail or Outlook.
  • Manage files and organize digital documents effectively using file storage systems.
  • Type efficiently, improving speed and accuracy for business communication and data entry.
  • Use basic internet security practices to safeguard your personal information and online activities.

Real-Life Applications of Basic Computer Knowledge

Mastering these basic computer skills will open numerous doors in both personal and professional settings. Here are a few ways to apply what you learn:

  • Office administration, where you can handle tasks like data entry, email communication, and document creation for small businesses or organizations.
  • Personal productivity, allowing you to use computer programs and applications to organize personal files, research online, and communicate digitally.
  • Freelancing opportunities such as content writing, virtual assistance, and data management, all of which require basic computer skills.
  • Academic success, where students can research, create assignments, and submit projects online using the tools and skills learned.
  • Small business operations, including creating business documents, managing emails, and organizing digital files for efficiency.

Benefits of the Basic Computer Knowledge Program

This program provides the foundational skills every professional or student needs to succeed in a digital world. Some key benefits include:

  • Confidence in navigating digital environments, including the internet, email, and software programs.
  • The ability to perform basic office tasks, making you more employable in administrative and support roles.
  • Improved efficiency and productivity, both in personal life and in the workplace.
  • Hands-on experience with popular software tools such as Microsoft Office, helping you create polished documents, presentations, and spreadsheets.
  • Preparation for more advanced digital skills or specific career-focused programs like cybersecurity, web development, or digital marketing.

Career Opportunities for Top Students

At Hamzury Innovation Hub, we reward excellence and provide support to our top-performing students. Students who excel in the Basic Computer Knowledge program will have access to opportunities for internships, mentorship, or guidance on further studies in tech-related fields.

This program is the first step toward gaining a strong foothold in today’s digital world and prepares you for a variety of roles or further specialized training.
