Program Overview

Welcome to Hamzury Innovation Hub, where we offer a specialized and cutting-edge program in Blockchain Technology, Forex Trading, and an Introduction to Web 3. This program is designed to provide students with an understanding of the foundational concepts of blockchain, the skills needed to trade in the forex market, and a glimpse into the future of the decentralized web. Before applying, ensure you have basic computer knowledge, as it is necessary for successful participation in this course.

Program Structure and Duration

Standard Blockchain, Forex Trading, and Web 3 Package

  • Duration: 3 months
  • Certificate awarded upon successful completion.
  • This program covers the basics of blockchain technology, forex trading strategies, and an introduction to Web 3 technologies like decentralized apps (dApps) and cryptocurrency.
  • Every four weeks, students will present their progress in team presentations.
  • In the final week, students will complete a capstone project, demonstrating their trading strategies or blockchain development skills.
  • All activities, including practicals, teamwork, and assignments, are compulsory.
  • Tuition fees for the standard package: 45,000 NGN.

Advanced Blockchain, Forex Trading, and Web 3 Package

  • This advanced program offers a deeper dive into smart contracts, decentralized finance (DeFi), advanced forex trading, and Web 3 integration.
  • Students will work with platforms like Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain, and MetaTrader, as well as gain experience with smart contract development and trading bots.
  • The advanced program prepares students for roles such as blockchain developers, forex traders, or DeFi experts.
  • Tuition fees for the advanced package: 70,000 NGN.

What You Will Be Able to Do After the Program

After completing the program, students will have a solid understanding of blockchain, forex trading, and Web 3 technologies. You will be able to:

  • Understand blockchain technology and its applications in finance, supply chain, healthcare, and other industries.
  • Develop and deploy smart contracts on platforms like Ethereum and Binance Smart Chain.
  • Trade forex markets using fundamental and technical analysis, including the use of trading platforms such as MetaTrader.
  • Apply Web 3 concepts to create decentralized applications (dApps) and understand how they interact with blockchain networks.
  • Analyze and interpret cryptocurrency markets and apply these skills in real-world trading scenarios.
  • Work in teams to design and implement blockchain-based solutions for business or personal projects.

Real-Life Applications of Blockchain Technology, Forex Trading, and Web 3

With the knowledge and skills gained from this program, students can explore various lucrative opportunities, including:

  • Freelancing as a blockchain developer or smart contract specialist for businesses venturing into blockchain technology.
  • Forex trading as a source of passive income by utilizing advanced trading strategies and market analysis.
  • Consulting for businesses interested in transitioning to Web 3 technologies and decentralized applications.
  • Crypto trading and DeFi participation, utilizing decentralized finance platforms to maximize returns.
  • Working with organizations in implementing blockchain solutions for more secure and transparent operations.
