Program Overview

Welcome to the Hamzury Innovation Hub, where we offer a practical and comprehensive program in Digital Marketing and Social Media Management. This program equips students with the knowledge and tools to excel in the fast-growing digital marketing industry and successfully manage social media platforms for businesses, brands, or personal projects. Before applying, ensure you have basic computer knowledge as it is essential for effective learning and project execution.

Program Structure and Duration

Standard Digital Marketing and Social Media Management Package

  • Duration: 3 months
  • Certificate awarded upon successful completion.
  • This course covers the fundamentals of digital marketing strategies, social media platforms, and content creation, with hands-on experience in campaign management and analytics.
  • Every four weeks, students will present their progress in team presentations.
  • In the final week, students will complete a capstone project, where they will design and execute a full-scale digital marketing campaign.
  • All activities, including practicals, teamwork, and assignments, are compulsory.
  • Tuition fees for the standard package: 45,000 NGN.

Advanced Digital Marketing and Social Media Management Package

  • This advanced program delves deeper into SEO (Search Engine Optimization), PPC (Pay-Per-Click) advertising, social media strategies, and analytics tools.
  • Students will gain experience in using platforms like Google Analytics, Facebook Ads, Instagram Marketing, and LinkedIn Advertising.
  • The advanced program prepares students for roles like digital marketing specialists, social media strategists, or content marketing managers.
  • Tuition fees for the advanced package: 70,000 NGN.

Innovation Hub Rules

  • Punctuality: Students must arrive on time for all classes and scheduled activities.
  • Active Participation: Full participation in all assignments, projects, and group activities is required.
  • Respectful Conduct: Respect must be shown to instructors, peers, and staff at all times.
  • Meet Deadlines: All assignments and projects must be completed and submitted on time.
  • Academic Integrity: Plagiarism or dishonest conduct will result in disciplinary action.
  • Professionalism: Professional conduct during all practical and teamwork sessions is expected.

What You Will Be Able to Do After the Program

Upon completing the program, students will have the skills and knowledge needed to develop and execute successful digital marketing and social media strategies. You will be able to:

  • Create and manage social media campaigns across platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, and TikTok.
  • Develop SEO strategies to improve website visibility and rankings on search engines.
  • Utilize PPC advertising and tools like Google Ads to run successful digital campaigns.
  • Analyze and report on marketing data using platforms like Google Analytics, Facebook Insights, and other analytics tools.
  • Create engaging content tailored to different audiences across various digital platforms.
  • Plan and execute a comprehensive digital marketing strategy for brands or businesses, including email marketing, influencer outreach, and social media management.

Real-Life Applications of Digital Marketing and Social Media Management

The skills gained from this program will open up numerous career opportunities in the digital marketing world. Here are a few real-life applications:

  • Freelance digital marketer, helping small businesses and brands develop and manage their online presence.
  • Social media management for businesses, influencers, or startups, handling their digital campaigns and community engagement.
  • SEO expert, working with companies to improve their online visibility and increase traffic through search engines.
  • Managing PPC campaigns and optimizing ads to drive results for e-commerce businesses or service providers.
  • Content creation for social media platforms, blogs, and websites, tailored to engage specific audiences and boost brand awareness.

Benefits of the Advanced Digital Marketing and Social Media Management Program

Our advanced program is ideal for students looking to specialize in the competitive field of digital marketing and social media strategy. Here are some benefits:

  • Master advanced marketing techniques such as retargeting, advanced keyword research, and multichannel marketing.
  • Learn how to implement AI-driven marketing tools to optimize social media and digital ad campaigns.
  • Build a strong portfolio showcasing your digital campaigns, SEO optimizations, and social media strategies.
  • Gain expertise in data-driven marketing, using advanced analytics to inform campaign decisions and strategies.
  • Prepare for in-demand roles such as digital marketing manager, social media strategist, or SEO consultant.

Upon completing the advanced program, you will be well-equipped to pursue a professional career in digital marketing and social media management, with the potential to work in-house, as a freelancer, or for a marketing agency.
