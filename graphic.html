Program Structure and Duration

Graphics Design and Animation Package

  • Duration: 4 months
  • Certificate awarded upon successful completion.
  • The course covers essential topics such as graphic design principles, animation techniques, software tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and After Effects.
  • Students will engage in practical hands-on sessions to ensure they can confidently create graphics and animations.
  • Every four weeks, students will have team presentations and collaborative projects to demonstrate their understanding of the material.
  • In the final week, students will complete a capstone project that brings together all their learning, such as creating a portfolio of their work.
  • All activities, including practicals, teamwork, and assignments, are compulsory.
  • Tuition fees for the program: 45,000 NGN.

What You Will Be Able to Do After the Program

By the end of the program, students will have mastered the essential skills needed to create professional graphics and animations. You will be able to:

  • Create visually appealing graphics using software tools like Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.
  • Develop animations using tools like Adobe After Effects.
  • Understand design principles and how to apply them to create effective visual content.
  • Build a professional portfolio showcasing your work.
  • Collaborate with teams to create comprehensive design projects.

Real-Life Applications of Graphics Design and Animation

Mastering these skills will open numerous doors in both personal and professional settings. Here are a few ways to apply what you learn:

  • Freelance graphic designer, creating visual content for clients.
  • Animator for media companies, creating animations for videos, advertisements, and more.
  • Content creator for social media, blogs, and websites, using your design skills to engage audiences.
  • Marketing and advertising, creating compelling visuals to promote products and services.
