Program Structure and Duration

IoT and Robotics Package

  • Duration: 4 months
  • Certificate awarded upon successful completion.
  • The course covers essential topics such as IoT fundamentals, robotics principles, sensor integration, microcontroller programming, and automation techniques.
  • Students will engage in practical hands-on sessions to ensure they can confidently work with IoT devices and robotic systems.
  • Every four weeks, students will have team presentations and collaborative projects to demonstrate their understanding of the material.
  • In the final week, students will complete a capstone project that brings together all their learning, such as developing an IoT solution or building a robotic system.
  • All activities, including practicals, teamwork, and assignments, are compulsory.
  • Tuition fees for the program: 45,000 NGN.

What You Will Be Able to Do After the Program

By the end of the program, students will have mastered the essential skills needed to work with IoT devices and robotic systems. You will be able to:

  • Understand IoT fundamentals and how to apply them in real-world scenarios.
  • Develop and program microcontroller-based systems for IoT and robotics applications.
  • Integrate sensors and actuators into IoT and robotic systems.
  • Design and build robotic systems for various applications.
  • Collaborate with teams to create comprehensive IoT and robotics projects.

  • Robotics engineer, designing and building robots for various applications.
  • Automation specialist, developing automated systems for manufacturing and other industries.
  • Research and development, working on cutting-edge projects in IoT and robotics.
