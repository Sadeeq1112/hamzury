Program Structure and Duration

Standard UI/UX Package

  • Duration: 3 months
  • Certificate awarded upon successful completion.
  • This course covers the fundamentals of UI/UX design, including user research, wireframing, prototyping, and user testing.
  • Every four weeks, students will present their progress in team presentations.
  • In the final week, students will complete a capstone project, where they will design and prototype a user interface for a real-world application.
  • All activities, including practicals, teamwork, and assignments, are compulsory.
  • Tuition fees for the standard package: 45,000 NGN.

Innovation Hub Rules

  • Punctuality: Be on time for all classes and scheduled sessions.
  • Active Participation: Students must fully engage in all assignments, group projects, and activities.
  • Respect for All: Treat instructors, peers, and staff with respect at all times.
  • Meet Deadlines: Assignments, projects, and tasks should be completed and submitted promptly.
  • Academic Integrity: Plagiarism and dishonesty are not tolerated; originality is expected.
  • Professional Behavior: Maintain professional conduct during all practicals and group work sessions.

What You Will Be Able to Do After the Program

By the end of the program, students will have mastered the essential UI/UX skills needed in various professional and academic fields. You will be able to:

  • Conduct user research to understand user needs and behaviors.
  • Create wireframes and prototypes to visualize and test design ideas.
  • Design intuitive and engaging user interfaces that provide a seamless user experience.
  • Conduct user testing to gather feedback and improve designs.
  • Use design tools such as Sketch, Figma, and Adobe XD to create high-fidelity designs.

Real-Life Applications of UI/UX

Mastering these UI/UX skills will open numerous doors in both personal and professional settings. Here are a few ways to apply what you learn:

  • UI/UX design for websites and mobile applications.
  • User research and testing for product development.
  • Freelancing opportunities in UI/UX design for various clients and projects.
  • Improving user experience for existing products and services.

Benefits of the UI/UX Program

This program provides the foundational skills every professional or student needs to succeed in the field of UI/UX design. Some key benefits include:

  • Confidence in designing user interfaces and experiences.
  • The ability to perform user research and testing.
  • Improved efficiency and productivity in design projects.
  • Hands-on experience with popular design tools such as Sketch, Figma, and Adobe XD.
  • Preparation for more advanced UI/UX skills or specific career-focused programs.

Career Opportunities for Top Students

At Hamzury Innovation Hub, we reward excellence and provide support to our top-performing students. Students who excel in the UI/UX program will have access to opportunities for internships, mentorship, or guidance on further studies in design-related fields.
